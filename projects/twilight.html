Twilight Mirror



During the early days of the pandemic I became increasingly interested in watching footage from live CCTV cameras based in random locations around the world. As each day blended into the other and the experience of time dilation became stranger, I wanted to engage with this compelling form of slow TV and develop ways of measuring change across minutes and days.

I began creating images that plot and compare the colours of twilight across different days as time unfolds. The following image was developed using footage from two subsequent evenings on a remote mountaintop in the Spanish desert.
