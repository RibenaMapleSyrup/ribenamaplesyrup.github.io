Facial Feedback



Some faces go viral, inspiring populations of doppelgängers to emerge who will undergo a spectrum of digital and cosmetic augmentations to emulate their idol.

The following composite portrait is made entirely from dozens of Kim Kardashian's doppelgängers, overlayed and fine-tuned to Kim’s face using facial landmark detection. Kim’s face becomes a focal lens with which to warp and smooth many other faces into a timeless collective identity.
